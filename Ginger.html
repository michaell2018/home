Gari

What is Gari?

Gari is a type of tsukemono (pickled vegetables). Gari is often served and eaten after sushi, and is sometimes called sushi ginger. It is considered to be essential in the presentation of sushi.

Ingredients

  • Ginger Root
  • Sugar
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Salt
